Echoes

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Echoes is designed with privacy as a core principle. Your voice memories are yours alone.

Data Collection

Echoes does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with third parties. We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools of any kind.

Microphone

Echoes uses the microphone solely to record voice memos at your explicit request. Audio files are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to external servers.

Location

Echoes uses GPS to anchor your voice memos to a specific place. Location data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted outside the app.

iCloud

If you have iCloud enabled on your device, Echoes may use your private iCloud storage to back up your data. This data is accessible only to you and is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Data Deletion

You can delete any Echo at any time from within the app. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data from your device.
